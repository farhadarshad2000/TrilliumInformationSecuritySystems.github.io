T-SOC Maturity Assessment Service

Level Up Your SOC

Our Offering

T-SOC Maturity Assessment Service helps clients make informed decisions about their cyber security investments, based on their current and targeted SOC maturity level. Our service is designed to help you assess the current level of preparedness, and the ideal path towards 5 levels of SOC maturity. It also ensures that the current policies and procedures are well-defined, subjectively evaluated, and implemented in line with industry leading practices.

HOW WE DO IT

T-SOC Maturity Assessment team calculates a maturity score using 5 maturity levels: ranging from level 0 which is non-existent SOC, up to level 5 which is highly capable/ quantitatively managed, measured across the domains: business, personnel, process, technology, and services
